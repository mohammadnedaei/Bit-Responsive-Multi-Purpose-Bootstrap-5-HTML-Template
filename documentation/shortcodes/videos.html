Videos

You can choose among 4 types of videos in your website.

  • Youtube Embed Video
  • Youtube Background Video
  • Lightbox Video
  • Local Background Video

-Youtube Embed Video:

Use any Youtube video by embed link.

Copy 
                                                                                    
<!-- Video -->
<div class="blog-media">
   <iframe width="640" height="360" class="radius responsive-video"
      src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/CMisZ4YtI3U" allowfullscreen></iframe>
</div>

-Youtube Background Video:

Use any Youtube video for background video.

Copy 
                                                                                    
<!--* Video BG Init Start -->
<div class="player"
   data-property="{videoURL:'https://youtu.be/QIs695Irf6Y',containment:'#home',autoPlay:true, showControls:true, showYTLogo: false, mute:true, startAt:0, opacity:1}">
</div>
<!--*  Video BG Init End -->

-Lightbox Video:

Use any video for your lightbox.

Copy 
                                                                                    
<a href="videos/video.mp4"
class="material-btn light-anim-grad-btn lightbox mfp-iframe mb-xs-20">
<i class="fas fa-play"></i>PLAY INTRO</a>

-Local Background Video:

Use your local video for background.

Copy 
                                                                                    
<!-- Home Section -->
<div class="local-video home-section full-height bg-dark-alfa-20" data-vide-bg="videos/video.mp4" id="home">
</div>
